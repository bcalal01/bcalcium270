Paci-Helios is a 2D game where you must defeat an AI hivemind and its entourage of fierce, ruthless drones. You, armed with nothing but your wits and agility, must use the enemies' firepower against themselves—dodge the barrage of astral projectiles and get your enemies to hit themselves or each other. Survive the fusillade and defeat the AI invaders to save the galaxy.
